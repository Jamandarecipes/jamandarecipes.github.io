Street Corn Bake

Ingredients

  • 2lbs bag frozen corn, thawed and roasted
  • .333333333333333333c sour cream
  • .333333333333333333c mayonnaise
  • 10oz queso fresco
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 2 teaspoon tajin
  • .25 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 lime

Procedure

  1. Preheat oven to 350F

  2. Roast corn in pan using butter or oil until slightly browned

  3. Bake for 20 minutes if using hot roasted corn. Bake time will be longer if using cold or frozen corn.

Yield: 4 Servings of Street Corn

Pepcorn

Ingredients

  • .5c popcorn kernels (trust the process, this will make a lot of popcorn)
  • .5 teaspoon theater salt(use a spice grinder to turn salt into powder)
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil (neutral flavored oil, like vegetable)
  • Optional: Kernel Season’s, or homemade seasonings to garnish
  • Optional: Gochujang

Procedure

  1. Place the popcorn kernel, salt, and oil into a large, high walled pot, or use a wok. Mix to ensure the salt, seasoning, or Gochujang fully dissolves and the kernels are fully covered in oil. Cover with a lid or aluminum foil (poke vent holes in aluminum).

  2. Turn heat to high and let sit until the first pop. Start moving the pot to swirl and agitate the kernels, if using a lid, hold on to the lid! Continue motion until there are 4-5 seconds between pops. Turn off heat and let sit until all popping has stopped, about 30-60 seconds.

  3. Serve in a large bowl. Optional: top with Kernel’s Season’s, or homemade powders.

Yield: 2 Servings of Popcorn

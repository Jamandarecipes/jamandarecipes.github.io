Tofu Schawarma

Ingredients

Tofu & Marinade

  • 1 Block of Tofu
  • .25c Soy Suace
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1.5 tablespoons lemon juice(or half a lemon)
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • .5 teaspoon cinnamon

Bread & Fillings

  • Honey goat cheese
  • Veggies of your choice. We used Red Onion, Tomato, Cucumber, & Spinach
  • Tzatziki
  • Pita or Naan

Procedure

Optional: Homemade Tzatziki
  • 1c Greek Yogurt
  • 2 tabespoons olive oil
  • 2 teaspoon dried dill
  • .5 of a large cucumber
  • .5 teaspoon salt
  1. Shred cucumber in cheese grater
  2. Mix all ingredients together in Bowl.

  1. Mix all marinade ingredients except Tofu together.

  2. Press Tofu to drain water.

  3. Shred Tofu in cheese grater.

  4. Put Tofu in bowl, pour marinade on it. Let sit for at least 2 hours.

  5. Set Oven / Air Fryer to 400 F.

  6. Bake Tofu until crispy. At least 35 minutes, but check every 20 or so.

  7. Load up your Bread with tofu, veggies, cheese, & sauce & enjoy!

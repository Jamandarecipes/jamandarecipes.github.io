Lemonade

Ingredients:

  • 1c sugar
  • 1c water
  • 9 lemons, more as needed
  • 6c water

Procedure:

  1. Make a simple syrup with the 1c sugar and 1c water.

  2. Juice 9 lemons into a gallon pitcher, strain out the seeds.

  3. Add the simple syrup and 6c of water. Taste and adjust with more water, sugar or lemons.

Variation: Strawberry Lemonade

Strawberry lemonade: puree 2lbs of strawberries, mix into lemonade, and strain out the pulp.

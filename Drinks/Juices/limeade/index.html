Ginger Limeade

Ingredients:

  • 12Limes
  • 0.75c honey
  • 0.25lbs ginger root
  • 7c water

Procedure:

  1. Peel ginger and chop into smaller pieces. Use a food processor or blender to pulverize the ginger root.

    1a. Optional: add 1/2c of water to help blender the ginger.

  2. Boil 4c of water and add your pulverized ginger. Steep for about 10mins. Add your honey to the hot ginger tea to fully dissolve.

  3. Juice your limes into a gallon container. Using a strainer, pour your ginger, honey tea into the line juice and add your remaining 4c water.

Variation: Soda

In step 3, use 4c of seltzer for a natural ginger lime soda.
